About

The Wasatch International Food Festival was formed from a community initiative to hold a food festival that would showcase the delicious ethnic foods from across the Wasatch Front. This two day festival will be held at the Utah Cultural Celebration Center Festival Grounds at 1355 West 3100 South West Valley City, UT 84119 on Friday, August 19th 5 p.m.-10 p.m. and Saturday, August 20th noon-10 p.m. The event will feature local food vendors serving international fair, a specialty foods market, family fun zone, live music, beer garden, a community mural project and food demonstrations.

Developed by West Valley City and opened in 2003, the Utah Cultural Celebration Center has evolved into one of Utah’s premier venues for arts and cultural events. West Valley City, Utah’s second largest, is a dynamic blend of ethnicities and cultural groups, and, as such, is a true reflection of the rich cultural tapestry of Salt Lake County.

Proceeds raised at the Wasatch International Food Festival will go to benefit the thousands of students, families and communities that participate in arts and cultural programs at the Utah Cultural Celebration Center.

For more information or to sign up for our e-news to get all the latest on upcoming events, programs, festivals visit http://www.culturalcelebration.org/

Get Involved

Support the Wasatch International Food Festival and the Utah Cultural Celebration Center and have fun doing it!

We are looking for community volunteers to help us with entrance and exit monitoring, ticket taking, ticket sales, Soft drink sales, Clean up, Hospitality (VIP area), Demo stage assistant, Community/Kids/Arts Projects, Information Booth, and more.

  • Volunteers will receive a free meal and complimentary entrace.
  • Volunteers must be 16+ years of age.
  • Shifts will be a 3 hour block on either Friday or Saturday.
