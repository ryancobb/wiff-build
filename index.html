About

The Wasatch International Food Festival was formed from a community initiative to hold a food festival that would showcase the delicious ethnic foods from across the Wasatch Front. This two day festival will be held at the Utah Cultural Celebration Center Festival Grounds at 1355 West 3100 South West Valley City, UT 84119 on Friday, August 19th 5 p.m.-10 p.m. and Saturday, August 20th noon-10 p.m. The event will feature local food vendors serving international fair, a specialty foods market, family fun zone, live music, beer garden, a community mural project and food demonstrations.

Developed by West Valley City and opened in 2003, the Utah Cultural Celebration Center has evolved into one of Utah’s premier venues for arts and cultural events. West Valley City, Utah’s second largest, is a dynamic blend of ethnicities and cultural groups, and, as such, is a true reflection of the rich cultural tapestry of Salt Lake County. The UCCC Foundation is a 501c3 non-profit that was created in 2012 to support UCCC programming and capital projects.

Proceeds raised at the Wasatch International Food Festival will go to benefit the thousands of students, families and communities that participate in arts and cultural programs at the Utah Cultural Celebration Center.

For more information visit http://www.culturalcelebration.org/uccc-foundation.html

Sponsors

The 2016 Wasatch International Food Festival is actively seeking sponsors. Align yourself with a great non-profit and reach your target market while having fun.

Benefits of sponsorship:

  • Product and specialty food demos on our kitchen stage
  • Reward employees and clients with VIP Tickets and tasting events
  • Expose your brand with signage & co-branded advertising
  • Oppurtunity to offer product demonstrations at the Demo Stage
  • Recognition at www.culturalcelebration.org
  • Supporting the local community through a nonprofit organization

Get Involved

Support the Utah Cultural Celebration Center and have fun doing it! The Wasatch International Food Festival is in need of volunteers both days to help with general operating tasks including: ticket sales, gate monitoring, event set up, recycling and trash clean up, kid zone, and more.

Volunteers will receive a food voucher, free water, a complimentary entrance ticket and a t-shirt.
